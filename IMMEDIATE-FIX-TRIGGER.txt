IMMEDIATE FIX: TRIGGER NOT FIRING
Quick 5-Minute Solution
================================================================================

PROBLEM:
--------
✅ Form has responses
❌ Make.com not triggering

================================================================================
QUICK FIX (5 MINUTES)
================================================================================

STEP 1: Reset Module 1 (2 min)
-------------------------------
1. Delete Module 1
2. Add new: Google Forms → Watch Responses
3. Select form
4. Save

STEP 2: Reconnect (1 min)
--------------------------
1. Click connection
2. Re-authenticate Google
3. Save

STEP 3: Turn ON (30 sec)
-------------------------
1. Turn scenario ON (green toggle)
2. Wait 30 seconds

STEP 4: Test (1 min)
---------------------
1. Submit NEW form response
2. Wait 2 minutes
3. Check execution history

================================================================================
MOST LIKELY FIX
================================================================================

RESET TRIGGER:
--------------
1. Delete Module 1
2. Add new Google Forms trigger
3. Reconnect
4. Turn scenario ON
5. Submit NEW response

This fixes 90% of trigger issues!
