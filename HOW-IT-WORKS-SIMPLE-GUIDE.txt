COMMON QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Do I need to do anything after adding a lead?
A: NO! Just add the lead. Emails send automatically.

Q: How long until Email 1 is sent?
A: Within seconds of adding the lead to Google Sheets.

Q: How long until Email 2 is sent?
A: 3 days after Email 1 (Scenario 2 runs daily and finds eligible leads).

Q: How long until Email 3 is sent?
A: 4 days after Email 2 (Scenario 3 runs daily and finds eligible leads).

Q: What if I add multiple leads at once?
A: Each lead gets processed automatically. Email 1 sends to all immediately.

Q: What if a lead's email is invalid?
A: Make.com will show an error in execution logs. The lead's status won't update.

Q: Can I add leads manually or import from CSV?
A: YES! Both work. Just make sure columns match the format.

Q: What if I want to skip Email 2 or Email 3 for a specific lead?
A: You can manually change Status to "Email 2 Sent" or "Email 3 Sent" to skip.
