STEP 1: FIX ROUTER LANGUAGE FILTERS (CRITICAL)
================================================================================

PROBLEM: Router is sending emails to both English and Arabic leads

SOLUTION: Add language filters to router routes

IN MAKE.COM UI:
---------------
1. Open Module 2 (Router)
2. Click on Route 1 (English)
3. Add Filter Condition:
   - Field: {{8.testCol3}} or {{1.3}} (Language column)
   - Operator: equals
   - Value: "en" (or "EN" - check your Google Sheets!)

4. Click on Route 2 (Arabic)
5. Add Filter Condition:
   - Field: {{8.testCol3}} or {{1.3}} (Language column)
   - Operator: equals
   - Value: "ar" (or "AR" - check your Google Sheets!)

VERIFY LANGUAGE VALUES:
-----------------------
1. Check Module 8 output (Set Variables)
2. Look at testCol3 value
3. Use the EXACT value (case-sensitive) in router filters

- Email 2 Date (H) = {{now}}

SHOULD BE (CORRECT):
- Status (E) = "Email 3 Sent" (or "Campaign Complete")

STEP 5: ADD SCHEDULE TRIGGER (OPTIONAL BUT RECOMMENDED)
================================================================================

To automate Email 3 sending:

1. Go to Scenario Settings (gear icon)
2. Add Schedule trigger
3. Configure:
   - Frequency: Daily
   - Time: 9:00 AM (or your preferred time)
   - Timezone: Your timezone

This will automatically run Email 3 daily and send to eligible leads.
