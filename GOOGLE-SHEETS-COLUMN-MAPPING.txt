Reference Guide for Email Automation

YOUR GOOGLE SHEETS STRUCTURE:
------------------------------

Column A (Index 0): Name
Column B (Index 1): Email
Column C (Index 2): Phone
Column D (Index 3): Language
Column E (Index 4): Status
Column F (Index 5): Date Added
Column G (Index 6): Email 1 Date
Column H (Index 7): Email 2 Date
Column I (Index 8): Email 3 Date
Column J (Index 9): Notes

================================================================================
MAKE.COM MODULE MAPPING
================================================================================

MODULE 1 (Google Sheets Watch Rows):
------------------------------------
Outputs data as:
- {{1.0}} = Name (Column A)
- {{1.1}} = Email (Column B)
- {{1.2}} = Phone (Column C)
- {{1.3}} = Language (Column D)
- {{1.4}} = Status (Column E)
- {{1.5}} = Date Added (Column F)
- {{1.6}} = Email 1 Date (Column G)
- {{1.7}} = Email 2 Date (Column H)
- {{1.8}} = Email 3 Date (Column I)
- {{1.9}} = Notes (Column J)

MODULE 8 (Set Variables):
-------------------------
Should map:
- name: {{1.0}} (Name from Column A)
- email: {{1.1}} (Email from Column B)
- language: {{1.3}} (Language from Column D)

Then outputs:
- {{8.name}} = Name
- {{8.email}} = Email
- {{8.language}} = Language

MODULE 3 & 4 (Send Email):
---------------------------
"To" field should use: {{8.email}}
Subject should use: {{8.name}}
Language filter: {{8.language}} = "en" or "ar"

MODULE 5 & 7 (Update Row):
---------------------------
Updates:
- Column E (Status): "Email 1 Sent"
- Column G (Email 1 Date): {{now}}

VERIFY YOUR SETTINGS

  Value: {{1.0}} ✅ (NOT {{1.`0`}})

  Value: {{1.1}} ✅ (NOT {{1.`1`}})

  Value: {{1.3}} ✅ (NOT {{1.`3`}})

Values to update:
  "4": "Email 1 Sent" ✅ (Column E - Status)
  "6": "{{now}}" ✅ (Column G - Email 1 Date)

================================================================================
COMMON ERRORS TO AVOID
================================================================================

❌ WRONG:
---------
- {{1.`1`}} (backticks)
- "8. email" (label, not variable)
- "8. name," (label, not variable)
- {{1.1}} in "To" field (should use {{8.email}})

✅ CORRECT:
-----------
- {{1.1}} (no backticks)
- {{8.email}} (variable from Module 8)
- {{8.name}} (variable from Module 8)
- {{8.language}} (variable from Module 8)
