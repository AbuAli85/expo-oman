================================================================================
IMAGE SETUP GUIDE
UAE Property Expo – Muscat Marketing Package
================================================================================

IMPORTANT: You need to add your poster images to make the templates work!

================================================================================
OPTION 1: For Landing Page (index.html)
================================================================================

1. Create an "images" folder in the same directory as index.html:
   expo-oman/
   ├── index.html
   ├── images/
   │   ├── uae-property-expo-english-poster.jpg
   │   └── uae-property-expo-arabic-poster.jpg

2. Place your images:
   - English poster → images/uae-property-expo-english-poster.jpg
   - Arabic poster → images/uae-property-expo-arabic-poster.jpg

3. The landing page will automatically load the images from the "images" folder.

================================================================================
OPTION 2: For Email Templates (all-email-templates.html)
================================================================================

⚠️ IMPORTANT: Email clients require images to be hosted online (URL), not local files!

You have two options:

A) HOST IMAGES ONLINE (Recommended):
   1. Upload your images to your website server:
      - www.uaepropertyexpo.ae/images/uae-property-expo-english-poster.jpg
      - www.uaepropertyexpo.ae/images/uae-property-expo-arabic-poster.jpg

   2. The email templates are already configured to use:
      - https://www.uaepropertyexpo.ae/images/uae-property-expo-english-poster.jpg
      - https://www.uaepropertyexpo.ae/images/uae-property-expo-arabic-poster.jpg

   3. If your images are hosted elsewhere, update the URLs in all-email-templates.html

B) USE IMAGE HOSTING SERVICE:
   - Upload to Imgur, Cloudinary, or similar service
   - Get the direct image URL
   - Replace the image URLs in all-email-templates.html

================================================================================
IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS (Recommended)
================================================================================

For best results:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Width: 600-1200px (for emails, 600px is optimal)
- File size: Under 200KB for emails (faster loading)
- Aspect ratio: Match your poster design
