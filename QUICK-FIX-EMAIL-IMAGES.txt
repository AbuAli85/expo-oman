================================================================================
QUICK FIX: Images Not Showing in Emails
================================================================================

PROBLEM: Email clients CANNOT load local paths like "mnt/data/..."
SOLUTION: Images MUST be hosted online with https:// URLs

================================================================================
IMMEDIATE FIX (3 Steps)
================================================================================

STEP 1: Host Your Images Online
---------------------------------
Option A - Your Website:
  1. Upload images to: www.thesmartpro.io/images/
  2. Get URLs like:
     - https://www.thesmartpro.io/images/poster-english.jpeg
     - https://www.thesmartpro.io/images/poster-arabic.jpeg

Option B - Free Image Hosting (Quick):
  1. Go to imgur.com
  2. Upload both poster images
  3. Right-click each image → "Copy image address"
  4. You'll get URLs like: https://i.imgur.com/abc123.jpeg


STEP 2: Update Email Templates
-------------------------------
Open all-email-templates.html and use Find & Replace (Ctrl+H):

REPLACEMENT 1 - English Images:
   Find: mnt/data/WhatsApp Image 2025-11-22 at 8.21.03 PM.jpeg
   Replace: [PASTE_YOUR_ENGLISH_IMAGE_URL_HERE]
   
   Example: https://www.thesmartpro.io/images/poster-english.jpeg

REPLACEMENT 2 - Arabic Images:
   Find: mnt/data/WhatsApp Image 2025-11-22 at 8.22.49 PM.jpeg
   Replace: [PASTE_YOUR_ARABIC_IMAGE_URL_HERE]
   
   Example: https://www.thesmartpro.io/images/poster-arabic.jpeg

Click "Replace All" for each replacement.


STEP 3: Test
-------------
1. Send test email to yourself
2. Check if images load
3. If yes → Ready to send!
4. If no → Check URL is accessible in browser


================================================================================
EXAMPLE: What URLs Should Look Like
================================================================================

❌ WRONG (Won't work in emails):
   mnt/data/WhatsApp Image 2025-11-22 at 8.21.03 PM.jpeg
   images/poster.jpg
   /mnt/data/image.jpeg

✅ CORRECT (Will work in emails):
   https://www.thesmartpro.io/images/poster-english.jpeg
   https://i.imgur.com/abc123xyz.jpeg
   https://res.cloudinary.com/yourname/image/upload/poster.jpg
