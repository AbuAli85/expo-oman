================================================================================
SOLUTION: Long Delays in Make.com (3 Days, 4 Days)
================================================================================

PROBLEM:
Make.com's util:FunctionSleep module has a maximum of 300 seconds (5 minutes).
You need 3 days (259,200 seconds) and 4 days (345,600 seconds).

================================================================================
SOLUTION OPTIONS
================================================================================

OPTION 1: Use Scheduled Scenarios (RECOMMENDED)
-------------------------------------------------

Instead of waiting in the same scenario, create SEPARATE scheduled scenarios
that check Google Sheets and send emails at the right time.

STRUCTURE:
----------

Scenario 1: "Email 1 - Invitation" (Triggered by Google Sheets)
├─ Send Email 1 (English or Arabic)
├─ Update Google Sheets: Status = "Email 1 Sent", Email 1 Date = now
└─ END

Scenario 2: "Email 2 - Reminder" (Scheduled to run daily)
├─ Trigger: Schedule → Every day at 9:00 AM
├─ Google Sheets → Search Rows
│   └─ Filter: Status = "Email 1 Sent" AND Email 1 Date = (3 days ago)
├─ Router → Check Language
│   ├─ Route 1 (EN): Send Email 2 English
│   └─ Route 2 (AR): Send Email 2 Arabic
├─ Update Google Sheets: Status = "Email 2 Sent", Email 2 Date = now
└─ END

Scenario 3: "Email 3 - Final Call" (Scheduled to run daily)
├─ Trigger: Schedule → Every day at 9:00 AM
├─ Google Sheets → Search Rows
│   └─ Filter: Status = "Email 2 Sent" AND Email 2 Date = (4 days ago)
├─ Router → Check Language
│   ├─ Route 1 (EN): Send Email 3 English
│   └─ Route 2 (AR): Send Email 3 Arabic
├─ Update Google Sheets: Status = "Campaign Complete", Email 3 Date = now
└─ END

HOW IT WORKS:
-------------
1. Scenario 1 sends Email 1 immediately when a new lead is added
2. Scenario 2 runs daily and checks for leads who received Email 1 exactly 3 days ago
3. Scenario 3 runs daily and checks for leads who received Email 2 exactly 4 days ago

This way, you don't need long waits - the scheduled scenarios handle the timing!


OPTION 2: Use Webhooks with External Delay Service
---------------------------------------------------

1. After Email 1, call a webhook URL that schedules Email 2 for 3 days later
2. Use a service like:
   - Zapier (has delay modules)
   - n8n (has delay nodes)
   - Custom webhook with cron job
   - Make.com webhook + external scheduler

This is more complex but works if you need everything in one scenario.


OPTION 3: Use Make.com's Schedule Module (Alternative)
--------------------------------------------------------

1. After Email 1, use Schedule → Schedule a Scenario
2. Schedule Email 2 scenario to run in 3 days
3. Pass the lead data via webhook or data store

This requires setting up webhooks to pass data between scenarios.


OPTION 4: Simplify - Use Shorter Delays for Testing
----------------------------------------------------

For testing purposes, you can use shorter delays:
- 3 days → 300 seconds (5 minutes) for testing
- 4 days → 600 seconds (10 minutes) for testing

Then change to scheduled scenarios for production.

RECOMMENDED: OPTION 1 - Scheduled Scenarios
================================================================================

This is the BEST approach for Make.com. Here's how to set it up:

STEP 1: Keep Your Current Scenario (Email 1)
--------------------------------------------
Your current scenario is perfect! Just remove the wait module.
It should:
1. Watch Google Sheets for new rows
2. Route by language
3. Send Email 1
4. Update Google Sheets: Status = "Email 1 Sent", Email 1 Date = now
5. END (no wait needed)


STEP 2: Create Scenario 2 - Email 2 Reminder
--------------------------------------------
1. Trigger: Schedule → Every day at 9:00 AM (or your preferred time)

2. Module 1: Google Sheets → Search Rows
   - Spreadsheet: Same as Scenario 1
   - Sheet: "leads"
   - Filter: 
     * Status (E) = "Email 1 Sent"
     * Email 1 Date (G) = (Today - 3 days)
   - Limit: 100 (or as many as needed)

3. Module 2: Router → Check Language
   - Route 1: Language (D) = "EN" → Send Email 2 English
   - Route 2: Language (D) = "AR" → Send Email 2 Arabic

4. Module 3: Google Sheets → Update Row
   - Update Status to "Email 2 Sent"
   - Update Email 2 Date (H) = now


STEP 3: Create Scenario 3 - Email 3 Final Call
-----------------------------------------------
1. Trigger: Schedule → Every day at 9:00 AM

2. Module 1: Google Sheets → Search Rows
   - Filter:
     * Status (E) = "Email 2 Sent"
     * Email 2 Date (H) = (Today - 4 days)

3. Module 2: Router → Check Language
   - Route 1: Language (D) = "EN" → Send Email 3 English
   - Route 2: Language (D) = "AR" → Send Email 3 Arabic

4. Module 3: Google Sheets → Update Row
   - Update Status to "Campaign Complete"
   - Update Email 3 Date (I) = now


STEP 4: Date Filtering in Google Sheets Search
------------------------------------------------
To find rows where Email 1 Date = 3 days ago:

In Make.com's Google Sheets Search module:
- Use a filter formula or calculated field
- Or use: Email 1 Date (G) is less than (now - 3 days)
- And: Email 1 Date (G) is greater than (now - 4 days)

This finds leads who received Email 1 exactly 3 days ago (within a 1-day window).

ALTERNATIVE: Use Make.com Data Store (Advanced)
================================================================================

If you want everything in one scenario flow:

1. After Email 1, store the lead data in Make.com Data Store
   - Key: Lead email + timestamp
   - Value: Lead data + scheduled send time

2. Create a separate scheduled scenario that:
   - Runs every hour
   - Checks Data Store for entries scheduled to send now
   - Sends Email 2 or Email 3
   - Deletes the entry from Data Store

This is more complex but keeps everything connected.
