================================================================================
COMMON ERRORS AND QUICK FIXES
================================================================================

ERROR: "Missing value of required parameter 'to'"
-------------------------------------------------
FIX:
1. Open Module 3 or 4 (Email module)
2. Change "To" field to: `[{"value": "{{1.1}}"}]`
3. Save and test again

ERROR: "The 'to' field must be a 'string'"
-------------------------------------------
FIX:
1. Open Module 3 or 4
2. Change "To" field to: `[{"value": "{{1.1}}"}]`
3. Save and test again

ERROR: "Filter condition not met"
---------------------------------
FIX:
1. Check Column D in Google Sheets - what value?
2. Open Module 3 or 4
3. Check filter - what does it check?
4. Make them match exactly (case-sensitive!)

ERROR: "Connection error"
------------------------
FIX:
1. Reconnect Google Sheets (Module 1)
2. Reconnect Resend (Module 3/4)
3. Test again
