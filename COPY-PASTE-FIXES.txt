MODULE 3 (English Email) - TO FIELD
================================================================================

EXACT FORMAT:
-------------
[{"value": "{{1.1}}"}]

NOT:
----
["{{1.1}}"]
["{{1.`1`}}"]
{{1.1}}

================================================================================
MODULE 3 (English Email) - FILTER
================================================================================

EXACT FORMAT:
-------------
Field: {{1.3}}
Operator: equals
Value: "en"

NOT:
----
{{1.`3`}}
"EN" (uppercase - unless your sheet uses uppercase)

QUICK REFERENCE
================================================================================

ALWAYS USE:
-----------
- {{1.0}} for Name (NO backticks)
- {{1.1}} for Email (NO backticks)
- {{1.3}} for Language (NO backticks)
- [{"value": "{{1.1}}"}] for To field

NEVER USE:
----------
- {{1.`0`}} (backticks)
- {{1.`1`}} (backticks)
- {{1.`3`}} (backticks)
- ["{{1.1}}"] (wrong format)
